About Us

Haven Steps Foundation is a Nairobi-based NGO co-founded by Maria Halkano and Ian Kinoti. We exist to restore hope and dignity by providing practical steps out of homelessness and street life. Our approach is holistic, combining care, training, and long-term support.

Our Programs

  • Emergency and transitional shelter
  • Vocational skills and job readiness training
  • Counseling and rehabilitation support
  • Reintegration and family tracing services
  • Health care access and hygiene services
